About the Lottery DApp

Welcome to the Lottery DApp platform! This decentralized application allows users to participate in a transparent and fair lottery system powered by blockchain technology. Managers can oversee the lottery, players can enter the draw, and winners are chosen randomly, ensuring fairness and trust.

Connect your wallet, join the game by paying the entry fee (1 ETH), and stand a chance to win big! The winner takes 70% of the pool, while the manager gets 30%.

Decentralized & Transparent

All transactions are secured and recorded on the Ethereum blockchain, ensuring transparency and fairness.

Randomized Winner Selection

Winners are chosen using a secure pseudo-random number generator built into the smart contract.

Manager Role

Only the designated manager can initiate the lottery and pick the winner, making it an organized process.
